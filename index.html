Who we are?

Welcome to the Center for Individual and Community Development through Scientific Research webpage! We are dedicated to promoting the development of individuals and communities, with a focus on marginalized groups such as disabled and uneducated individuals from the Dalit community. Through our scientific research, we strive to improve their lives and contribute to the betterment of society as a whole. Explore the sections below to learn more about our research areas and initiatives.

What we do?

Tissue Culture: In this section, we conduct research on tissue culture techniques for various species, including orchids, fruits, and bananas. We aim to enhance the production and distribution of these plants through advanced tissue culture methods. Accompanied by vivid pictures and diagrams, this section showcases our expertise in tissue culture and its potential benefits.


DNA banks and cell line banks: Learn about our initiatives in DNA banks and cell line banks. We work towards preserving genetic materials and cell lines to facilitate future research and scientific advancements. This section provides insights into the importance of maintaining these repositories and the potential they hold for future discoveries. Images depict the storage and preservation techniques.
