Who we are?

Welcome to the "Center for Molecular Biosciences" webpage! for Individual and Community Development through Scientific Research. We are dedicated to promoting the development of individuals and communities, with a focus on marginalised groups such as disabled and uneducated individuals from the unprivileged class. Through our scientific research, we strive to improve their lives and contribute to the betterment of society as a whole by using our local resources, plant products, water resources and probiotics.

What we do?

Tissue Culture: Unlock the potential of tissue culture techniques through our cutting-edge research. Our expertise spans various species, and our captivating visuals demonstrate the remarkable benefits it offers.

Rare Disease Studies: Join us in our mission to unravel the mysteries of genetic disorders. From Sickle Cell Anaemia to Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, and Epilepsy, we explore the intricate genetic diversity across animal species. Through scientific investigations, we aim to advance our understanding and contribute to innovative treatments. Explore our research process and outcomes through captivating images and diagrams.

Utilization of Plant Extracts: Discover the endless possibilities of plant extracts for local commercialization. By leveraging community resources, we explore their potential in developing drugs, perfumes, oils, sanitary towels, and even extracting drinking water from tree sap. Our research aims to harness the power of natural resources for diverse applications.

DNA banks and cell line banks: Unveil the future of scientific research through our DNA and cell line banks. We are dedicated to preserving vital genetic materials, facilitating breakthroughs and advancements in various fields. Explore the significance of maintaining these repositories and the boundless potential they hold for future discoveries.

Study of Bacteria, Fungi, and Plant Diseases: Delve into our research on plant diseases and their effective remedies. Our findings, methodologies, and their direct implications for agricultural practices are presented in this section. Stay ahead with our innovative solutions, supported by compelling evidence and practical insights.
